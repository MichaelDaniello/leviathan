Major Powers
The Combine
Corporate Mega-State · Core Systems
Owns most of core space. Not evil per se — just profit-driven to the point where 'evil' is a rounding error. They build everything, own everything, and charge interest on everything else.
The Sovereignty
Government · Old Colonies
What remains of Earth's original colonial governments. They have laws, courts, and a navy. They also have corruption, bureaucracy, and a losing war against irrelevance.
The Syndicates
Criminal Networks · Everywhere
Organized crime that's often more organized than the governments. They run the black markets, the smuggling lanes, and the jobs that pay well because legitimate contractors won't touch them.
Church of the Architects
Religious Organization · Scattered
Believes alien precursors left technology and messages across the galaxy. They might be right. They're definitely insufferable about it. Part archaeologists, part cultists, part treasure hunters.
